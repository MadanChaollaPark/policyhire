# Feature Parity And Differentiation Checklist

## Candidate Product

- Keyword, location, policy area, category, contract, seniority, work mode, language, salary, deadline, and posted-date filters.
- Job cards with employer logo, verified badge, salary signal, work mode, contract, policy tags, deadline status, and save action.
- Job detail pages with summary, responsibilities, requirements, benefits, salary, deadline, right-to-work note, apply method, employer profile, and related match reasons.
- Saved jobs, anonymous-to-account migration path, hidden jobs, already-applied state, and application history.
- Alerts by keyword, employer, policy area, salary band, seniority, work mode, and frequency: instant, daily, weekly.
- CV/profile upload with EU affairs fields: policy areas, languages, institution experience, right-to-work status, and public/private visibility.
- Internship calendar for Blue Book, Schuman, agencies, party groups, NGOs, embassies, and recurring Brussels traineeships.

## Recruiter Product

- Guided job-post form with title, company, category, contract, seniority, salary, city, country, work mode, deadline, apply URL/email, contacts, and policy tags.
- Salary transparency prompts, unpaid-internship checks, duplicate detection, vague seniority warnings, and deadline validation.
- Employer dashboard for drafts, publishing, edits, renewals, bulk posting, hosted apply forms, ATS links, invoices, and campaign add-ons.
- Analytics for views, apply clicks, saves, alert impressions, newsletter reach, source channels, conversion, and category benchmarks.
- Pricing tiers for basic listings, featured campaigns, recruiter suite, newsletter sponsorship, social promotion, and custom packages.

## Market Intelligence

- Verified employer directory with open-role counts, organisation type, policy domains, salary transparency, hiring velocity, and quality badges.
- Transparency-register entity pages with goals, interests, country, registration metadata, spend band, related jobs, and career guides.
- Brussels Best style supplier directory for consultancies, law firms, intelligence tools, trainers, specialists, and public-affairs vendors.
- Salary explorer by role, seniority, policy area, organisation type, city, contract type, and sample size.
- Candidate-market insights for recruiters across seniority, languages, compensation, and policy-demand signals.

## SEO And Trust

- Structured data for `JobPosting`, `Organization`, `BreadcrumbList`, `Article`, and `FAQPage`.
- Programmatic landing pages for locations, policy areas, categories, organisation types, seniority, languages, and internships.
- Sitemap, robots rules, canonical URLs, Open Graph, Twitter cards, manifest, favicon, and crawl-safe expired-job handling.
- Public methodology for audience claims, salary data, duplicate detection, job freshness, and employer verification.
