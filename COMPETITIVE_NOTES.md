# Competitive Notes

## EUJobs.co

- Strong IA: jobs, companies, lobbying entities, Brussels Best, Fair Pay, blog, alerts, and employer posting.
- Pricing baseline: low-cost basic listing, pro package with featured/newsletter/social/analytics, recruiter package with longer duration and bulk tooling.
- Strong SEO footprint through company pages, entity pages, career guides, blog, sitemap, and structured data.
- Gaps to beat: inconsistent imported job data, shallow salary normalization, directory-heavy entity discovery, and limited guided-posting controls.

## EuroBrussels

- Mature EU affairs taxonomy with policy area, job type, workplace, country, experience, alerts, CV database, newsletter, education, and career content.
- Premium recruiter packages include front-page placement, newsletter distribution, alerts, ATS, pre-selection questions, and account manager support.
- Gaps to beat: legacy dense UI, conflicting audience claims, friction-heavy alert signup, and inconsistent saved-job experiences.

## Jobs in Brussels

- Strong Brussels positioning, organisation directory, internship calendar, category routes, salary estimates, and mission-driven employer focus.
- Gaps to beat: shallow search/filtering, weak saved-search controls, limited salary and seniority facets, and fewer preference-rich alerts.

## Euractiv Jobs

- Strong top-employer surface, categories, jobletter funnel, recruitment packages, and market-content moat.
- Gaps to beat: WordPress-style layout, inconsistent employer data, hidden salary labels, thin category counts, and underdeveloped shortlisting.
